I can't stop.

I don’t like feeling this way Trying to forget about her because Well...that’s the only way i know to heal myself But I doubt any healing will come because I’ve taken the strongest hit of beautiful And my eyes can’t get pumped I can’t stop thinking about her Why me? I really don’t miss this feeling I can’t stop thinking about her.

Only. Only if she would let me be hers And her mine It’ll all be fine.

If you really want something, they say “Never give up” That’s what I’m going to do. If you love something, they say “Let it go” That’s what I’ll have to do eventually.
