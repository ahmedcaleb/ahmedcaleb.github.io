I can't stop.

Posted by Chips on January 1st, 2019

I can't stop.

I don’t like feeling this way, trying to forget about her because...

... well, that’s the only way i know to heal myself. But I doubt any healing will come because I’ve taken the strongest hit of beautiful,

and my eyes can’t get pumped. I can’t stop thinking about her! Why me?

I really don’t miss this feeling, I can’t stop thinking about her.

Only. Only if she would let me be hers, and her mine, It’ll all be fine.

If you really want something, they say- “Never give up” that’s what I’m going to do.

If you love something, they say- “Let it go” that’s what I’ll have to do eventually.
