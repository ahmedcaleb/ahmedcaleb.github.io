It's complicated

Posted by Chips on January 17th, 2019

This might be the last time I say I love you Feelings hit hard and fade away slowly. Commitment isn’t easy to come by, But when I see something worth fighting for, I commit. You were worth every single second. You were worth every single smile. You were worth every single moment I spent with you, But there are things I never told you

I hated when we started ignoring each other. "I will always be there"- Those words are inscribed on the walls of my heart just so I never forget, And trust me, I will never forget.

Just tell me how you feel . When we are together, It feels like the happiest moment ever. When we’re not, It feels like we’re strangers. It confuses me, And I don’t want to give up on you without knowing what I might already know.

You see, a part of me tells me you love me, And a part of me keeps saying you never did. I could choose to believe either of these thoughts, But ultimately, it all depends on you.

I could write and tell you things Because that’s the only way I know how to express myself without having second thoughts. Maybe I should just say it. When I’m with you all I am is happy.

I’ve dwelled on the past for so long. I’m hoping for a better future without anymore heartbreaks, But that’s inevitable. I can only accept it, Because people always disappoint.

So here I am with my heart still on the floor, Shattered into a million pieces. Still waiting to see if you pick up any piece And I will love you with the rest of my broken heart.
