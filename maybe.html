I can't stop.

Posted by Chips, written by mark_my_words on January 1st, 2019

Maybe.

Maybe is was, maybe it wasn't, maybe i lied, maybe you just believed what you wanted.

I guess you can just look at me like i don't deserve it. And yeah, i have gone for the cheapest happiness and cheapest love i could find, maybe I'm just that lonely and scared.

All I need is a place to unload my heavy heart and fill my empty soul. All i desire is a place to cry out my actions and sorrows, then maybe, just maybe I could forget the consequences of my actions and move on from there.
