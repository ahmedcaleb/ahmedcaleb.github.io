I was about to break

Posted by Chips on December 29, 2018

I was about to break It was just a second that made the difference

It was me or my feelings. It was my mental health or my emotions.

I let go and waited for a sign Even if it’ll be a sign of a feeling Still... Nothing.

I hate feeling like this for another person. Don’t @ me You just made me fall deeper But it’s all okay now I guess it’s true, You feel so much till you can’t feel anything But I lied And I’ve broken a promise I can’t love you forever And I’ve stopped myself from loving you

I can’t hurt myself waiting for you to see me I can’t numb myself, waiting for you to love me like I love you I know you have demons that’s not letting you love right now But don’t ever forget, everybody’s got demons All I wanted was for us to deal with them together. I guess it’s too late for either of us to love again.
