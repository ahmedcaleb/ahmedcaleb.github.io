Illusions.

Posted by Chips, written by mark_my_words on January 7th, 2019

Despair.

We were supposed to be perfect. Until the perfect "we" became the perfect "me". I still don't understand what happened, cause it went so fast and I'm stuck in the mirage of our happiness over and over again.

You were my greatest lie, telling me you won't leave. You were my deepest thoughts, always on my mind. You stole my precious time, wasted on a stone. All I wanted was happiness and I thought I saw it wrapped in your soul.

Now nothing seems to make sense, we had it all and you still went outside to find a little more. Was I not enought? Why would you crack something so magical to walk with something so normal.
